## Judge Q&A Quick Answers

Q: Where is AI in this project?
A: The AI layer is the contextual risk engine that combines weather, night/day, traffic simulation, and hotspot proximity to output risk label, risk percentage, confidence, and factor-wise reasons.

Q: Is this deployable?
A: Yes. It is a frontend-only deployable app, runs with npm install and npm run dev, and can be deployed on Vercel/Netlify.

Q: How is this useful in India specifically?
A: India emergency helplines (108/100/101), India-biased route/place search, and practical risk-first commute decisions for dense urban traffic.
